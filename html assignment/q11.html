IN HTML element ARE GENRALLY categorized into two type block lavel element and in line element .
1 = block level element=
*layout = block level element start a new line and occupy the full width.
streching from the left to right of the container
*example=
div. p . h1 . ul. li . section
*advantages=
used to for creating overall structure of web page.
2 = inline element =
*layout = inline element do not start on a new line and only take up as much width as necessary .
*behavior = they do not disrupt the flow of content
they sit within the line of text or other inline element.
*example=
span. a. strong .em. img.
*advantages = used for styling and formatting parts of block level element
